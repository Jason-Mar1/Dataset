FP60 Forestry Pest Dataset Introduction FP60林业害虫数据集介绍

Dataset Background 数据集背景

Since there is currently no publicly available large-scale dataset for forestry pests, this project constructed the FP60 dataset, covering the distribution of common forestry pests, including 60 species from 15 biological families. 由于当前没有公开的大规模林业有害生物数据集，因此本项目构建了覆盖常见林业害虫分布情况的FP60数据集，涵盖了15个生物科下的60种林业害虫。

Dataset Construction Process 数据集构建过程

We used the Latin scientific name of each insect as search keywords to retrieve images from common search engines like Google, Bing, and Baidu. The search results were manually screened to filter out low-quality images. During the collection process, we established three datasets according to the biological family hierarchy: 我们使用每个昆虫的拉丁学名作为搜索关键词，通过谷歌、必应、百度等常见的图像搜索引擎进行检索，并对检索结果进行人工筛选，以过滤低质量图像。在收集过程中，我们按照生物科层次结构建立了三个数据集：

  • FP15: Contains 15 forestry pest species from 10 different biological families, totaling 1735 images. 包含10类不同生物科共15种林业害虫，共1735张图像。
  • FP40: Contains 40 forestry pest species from 10 different biological families, totaling 3087 images. 包含10类不同生物科共40种林业害虫，共3087张图像。
  • FP60: Contains 60 forestry pest species from 15 different biological families, totaling 7253 images. 包含15类不同生物科共60种林业害虫，共7253张图像。

FP60 Dataset Detailed Distribution FP60 数据集详细分布

The FP60 dataset includes 60 pest species from 15 biological families, totaling 7253 high-quality forestry pest images. Image sizes range from 640×480 to 6016×4016 pixels, with an average size of 1248×957 pixels. The number of images per forestry pest species ranges from 72 to 236. FP60数据集包含15个生物科下的60种害虫，总计7253张高质量林业害虫图像。图像大小从640×480到6016×4016不等，平均大小为1248×957像素。每种林业害虫图像的数量在72张到236张之间。

Data Annotation 数据标注

For the forestry pest images, we used the Labelling software to annotate the location of the pests in the image and the biological family information to which the pest belongs, while also recording the species information of the pest in the image. The dataset format is: 对于林业害虫图像，我们通过使用Labelling软件来标注害虫在图像中的位置和该害虫所属的生物科信息，同时记录该图像中害虫的种类信息。数据集形式为：

D = {(Xi, Bi, fi, si)}i=1^N

Where Xi is the original pest image, Bi ∈ R^(4×ni) represents the bounding boxes of the pests in Xi, ni is the number of pests in Xi, fi ∈ R^ni and si ∈ R^ni are the family and species label vectors of Xi, respectively, and N is the number of pest images in the dataset. 其中Xi是原始害虫图像，Bi ∈ R^(4×ni)表示Xi中害虫的边界框，ni为Xi中害虫的数量，fi ∈ R^ni和si ∈ R^ni分别是Xi的生物科和物种标签向量，N是数据集害虫图像的数量。
